Bucket List Calculator Tutorial


Welcome to the Bucket List Calculator Tutorial. You are viewing this page because you are one of our new users or because somewhere along the line you got confused and wanted to learn a bit more about how this site works. Let me start by saying congrats on getting started, you have made a huge step in the right direction to completing all your life goals.

What is Bucket List Calculator


The most important thing to understand about Bucket List Calculator is that it is a tool. As with any tool unless you understand how the tool works and learn to use it properly your gonna have a bad time. It is very important for you to understand how to input data about your goals correctly in order for relevant statistics to be output for you.


Bucket List Calculator also has a lot of ways that you can interact with other users. We put this in place so you can see what kind of goals other users have and how difficult they believe their goals will be to accomplish. Plus it's a great way to get awesome goal ideas!


How to use Bucket List Calculator


Since the main purpose of Bucket List Calculator is to give you stats how difficult your goals will be to accomplish its really important to know how to input information about yourself and your goals.

  • Setting Up Your Profile

    Your profile has four important pieces of information to fill out

    • Birth Date
    • Age Done
    • Yearly Income
    • Hourly Wage

  • Creating Goal Items

    Each Bucket List Item has four attributes

    • Goal Types
    • Cost
    • Hours
    • Days

  • Stats and Recommendations

    This is the bread an butter of the website. This outputs all of the cool statistics that are created from the information you have entered
